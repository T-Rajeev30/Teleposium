About The Event

Teleposium, organized by the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE), is an annual flagship techno Cultural fest designed for innovation, creativity and collaboration.

With 10+ thrilling competitions, participants can showcase their skills across various domains:

🚀 Circuit Debugging 🎙️ JAM ❓ Technical Quiz 🤖 Robothon 🎮 Digital Adventure (BGMI) 📜 Paper Presentation 🔍 Treasure Hunt 🛠️ Mini Project 🎭 Turncoat 💻 Web/App Development

Compete, network, and win exciting prizes while pushing the boundaries of technology and innovation!

FAQs

Who can participate?

The competition is open to students, professionals or innovators and participants can register individually or in teams.

How do I register for the event?

You can register through our official website by filling out the registration form and submitting the required details.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, participants can register for multiple events as long as the event timings do not clash.

What are the judging criteria for the events?

The judging criteria vary for each event but generally include creativity, technical skills, problem-solving ability, and presentation.

What is the team size limit for team-based events?

The team size varies by event.

Will certificates be provided to all participants?

Yes, all participants will receive a participation certificate, and winners will receive special merit certificates.
