Poster Presentation

  • Individual participant only.
  • Poster must be presented in the form of Hard copy.
  • Soft copies are not considered.
  • Posters must be related to electronics and technology.
  • The size of the poster should be A1 (23.4 x 33.1 inches) or A2 (16.5 x 23.4 inches).
  • Posters must be digitally printed.
  • Each team will get 8 minutes to present their poster.
  • Judges may ask questions based on the presentation

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Content Relevance - The topic should be related to electronics/technology.
  • Creativity & Design - Visual appeal and clarity of information
  • Presentation Skills - How well the topic is explained.

General Rules:

  • Posters must be original and not copied from other sources.
  • Any form of plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
